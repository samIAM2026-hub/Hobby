02 Narrative & cards

This specimen shows the original dashboard aesthetic: Inter as the single typeface across headings, body and data, paired with a navy gradient hero and a coral-red accent. Cards carry a top accent bar, soft shadows and a 14px corner radius.

Card component

Rounded surface, hairline border, soft shadow, and a colored top rule that signals category. The hover lift and category accents (blue, teal, amber, purple, green) define the visual system.
